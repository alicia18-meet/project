Featured Galleries


Zemack Contemporary Art (ZCA) Tel Aviv




ZCA aims to deliver a fresh perspective on contemporary art, created by established Israeli and international artists. Since its foundation in 2010, ZCA has had a notable impact on the Israeli art scene, and is currently considered one of the leading galleries in Tel Aviv.




The Walled Off Hotel by Banksy, Bethlehem



The famous British artist Banksy is back with a brand new project called “The Walled Off Hotel,” and people can literally sleep inside this work of art. Located in Bethlehem, it offers the ‘worst view in the world’ with windows overlooking the controversial wall that separates the West Bank from Israel. The Walled Off hotel is itself a whole gallery, and presents political and contemporary art.
